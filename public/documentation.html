Documentation myFlix

INSTRUCTIONS:

This app is designed to navigate you in the world of best movies made!

Here you will find information about different movies, directors, and genres.

You can sign up, update personal info, and create a list of favorite movies.

This is the Documentation for myFlix

This is an application that will let you sort through different genres of movies
and directors, and let you save your favorite movies to your account.
